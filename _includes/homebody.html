Ceptr's Key Sub-Projects

It will be easier to understand Ceptr after digging into some of the key technologies it runs on:

Protocol for Pluggable Protocols

A central tenet for Ceptr is that anything can talk to anything. No more silos or brittle APIs. Self-Describing protocols and a universal parsing system are at the foundation of interoperability.

Holographic Storage on Holochains

Beyond Blockchain and global consensus ledgers is a practical and scalable architecture for ensuring data integrity and sharing it effectively.

Distributed Apps -- Dew Net

Other platforms are claiming to provide means for distributed computing, but Ceptr takes it to a whole new level.
